AISX Lab, Louisiana State University – Faculty Advisor: Dr. James Ghawaly | Research Collaborator: Jackson Descant

Overview

PECAN (Programming Encoder Classification Analysis Network) is my LSU AISX Lab research project on lightweight programming language identification. This page gives a quick context, while the poster and thesis draft provide the full methodology, experiments, and results.

Research Focus: Accurate language identification with compact models designed for practical use.
